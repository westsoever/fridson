## Judging Criteria

**1. PRODUCT** — How strong, functional is the product itself?

**2. PROBLEM** — Is it solving an actual pain and is it doing so in a new or original way?

**3. VALIDATION** — What evidence/signals are there that there's an actual market for this?

**4. SPEED & DEMO** — How far did you get with the limited time and resources and did you present a catchy & entertaining demo?
