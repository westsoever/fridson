# Hackathon Context

Load the hackathon judging criteria at the start of every session so every decision stays aligned with what wins.

## Judging Criteria

**1. PRODUCT** — How strong, functional is the product itself?

**2. PROBLEM** — Is it solving an actual pain and is it doing so in a new or original way?

**3. VALIDATION** — What evidence/signals are there that there's an actual market for this?

**4. SPEED & DEMO** — How far did you get with the limited time and resources and did you present a catchy & entertaining demo?

## Scoring Checklist
Before any major decision, ask:
- [ ] Does this make the product stronger and more functional?
- [ ] Does it address a real, validated pain point in a novel way?
- [ ] Do we have signals/evidence of market demand?
- [ ] Does this help us demo faster and more compellingly by Sunday 16:00?

## How to use this in-session

After loading, remind yourself and the user:
- Every feature decision → does it make the **product stronger**?
- Every design choice → does it sharpen the **problem narrative**?
- Every data point gathered → is it **validation** for market demand?
- Every hour spent → are you moving toward a **demoable, catchy** story?

When reviewing code, features, or plans, score the current state against all four criteria and flag the weakest one as the next focus.
